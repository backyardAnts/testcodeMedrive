About Us

Founded in 1924, Joubran Acar Ets. began by importing industrial supplies. In 1933, it became Joubran Acar & Fils, adding a mechanical workshop and representing international electromechanical brands in Lebanon.

In 1970, the company became a limited liability firm and secured exclusive rights to SEW-EURODRIVE products. In 2008, Middle-East Drives SAL (Offshore) was created to expand into the Middle East, quickly gaining the trust of Arab customers.
