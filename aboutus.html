Trusted in industrial supplies since 1924,
serving Lebanon and the Middle East

1924

Joubran Acar Ets.

Joubran Acar Ets. were founded in 1924. Their main activity was the import and distribution of industrial supplies.

1933

Joubran Acar & Fils

In 1933 a new company is created under the name of Joubran Acar & Fils and adds to the Company a mechanical workshop. It represents exclusively on the Lebanese market several international companies in the electromechanical industrial field.

1970's

Company Growths

In 1970, the company grew and became a limited liability company. Three years later, it acquires the exclusive rights of USOCOME SA, the French branch of SEW-EURODRIVE, and imports, distributes, maintains, and repairs the company’s products in Lebanon.

2008

The Born of Middle-East Drives SAL

To reach the Middle East market, a second company, partner of SEW-EURODRIVE GmbH, was created in 2008 under the name Middle-East Drives SAL (Offshore). Essentially developed for export from the SEW-EURODRIVE factories in Germany, France, Finland and China, Middle East Drives SAL (Offshore) acquired the confidence of its Arab customers in a short time.

SEW-EURODRIVE

Founded in 1931, SEW-EURODRIVE is a family company that started in the manufacture of mechanical speed reducers for the German industry, until 1945 with the arrival of Ernst Blickle at the head of the company, who instills a new dynamic through the manufacture of electric motors. Today SEW-EURODRIVE is the world leader in training technology and motion management solutions for industrial systems, with a presence in 51 countries, 15 manufacturing sites around the world, more than 16,000 employees and 2.8 billion €uro turnover.Products and systems from SEW-EURODRIVE are used all over the world: In industries such as automotive, building and building materials, beverage or food and consumer goods, metal processing or wood processing, transportation and logistics, in ports and airports, and many more.
